About Chess

Chess is a two player strategy board game played on a checkered board with 64 squares arranged in an 8×8 grid.It is one of the world's most popular games, played by millions of people worldwide at home, in clubs, online,by correspondence, and in tournaments. Chess is believed to have originated in India around the 6th century Ad and has evolved over the centuries into the modern game we know today.
