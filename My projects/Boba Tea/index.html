Bubble Tea

Introduction

Boba tea,or bubble tea is a tasty and refreshing drink. It's made with tea, milk, sugar, and those chewy tapioca balls we all love. People around the world enjoy it, and you can mix and match tons of flavors and toppings to make it just how you like it.

History of Boba Tea

So, boba tea came from Taiwan back in the '80s. The story goes that someone mixed tapioca pearls into iced milk tea, which made for a cool, chewy drink. It got super popular in Asia and then took off worldwide.
