header: Used to represent the top section of a web page, often containing headings, logos, and navigation.

nav: Signifies a navigation menu on a web page.

footer: Represents the footer of a web page, usually containing copyright information and contact details. figure and figcaption: Used for embedding images, diagrams, or charts, along with a caption.
